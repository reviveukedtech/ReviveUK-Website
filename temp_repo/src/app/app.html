Education for Everyone

ReviveUK EdTech is a Community Interest Company (CIC). All our profits are reinvested back into the community.

Our purpose is to advance education, relieve poverty, and reduce social exclusion. We support individuals facing financial hardship, neurodivergent learners, and those seeking new opportunities.

  • Improved access to inclusive education
  • Clear progression into training and jobs
  • Greater income stability for families
