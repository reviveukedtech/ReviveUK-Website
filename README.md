# ReviveUK EdTech Website

This repository contains the frontend for the **ReviveUK EdTech CIC** digital platform. We are a community-interest company dedicated to providing inclusive STEAM education and employability support to disadvantaged and underserved individuals in **Lancaster** and surrounding areas.

Our goal is to leverage technology to reduce poverty and social exclusion by improving access to skills, training, and clear pathways into skilled employment.

---

## 🌟 The Mission
The platform is designed to serve as a holistic hub for:
* **Inclusive STEAM Education:** Delivering accessible learning in coding, robotics, digital technology, and applied science.
* **SEND & SEMH Support:** Providing personalized sessions and adapted delivery for learners with **Special Educational Needs and Disabilities (SEND)** and **Social, Emotional and Mental Health (SEMH)** needs.
* **Holistic Assessment:** Utilizing a digital platform to identify educational, wellbeing, and practical support needs using **Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs** as a guiding framework.
* **Digital Signposting:** Providing accessible information on education, training, welfare, health, and employment services.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Architecture
To ensure a high-performance, accessible experience, this project is built using:

* **Framework:** **Angular** utilizing the latest **Signals** for reactive state management.
* **Layout:** A modern **Bento-grid** modular structure for visual predictability and cognitive ease.
* **Theming:** A CSS variable-based system supporting **Dark Mode** (Desaturated Navy) and **Light Mode** (Cloud Dancer).
* **Accessibility:** Built with **Angular CDK `a11y`** and high-contrast typography (Cream #FFFAF0 on dark surfaces) to meet WCAG standards.

---

## ♿ Accessibility First
Accessibility isn't a feature; it's our foundation. We specifically optimize for SEND/SEMH audiences by:
* **Reducing Cognitive Load:** Using progressive disclosure and predictable navigation.
* **Sensory Management:** Respecting `prefers-reduced-motion` and avoiding aggressive, high-frequency visual stimuli.
* **Clear Communication:** Implementing the **Angular CDK `LiveAnnouncer`** for screen reader notifications and using plain language across the UI.
